How does it work for a student

5 easy steps!
  1. Register
  2. Fill in all the needed info
  3. Look for the interesting internships yourself
  4. If you don't find any, enjoy our fun facts on the front page and we will contact you as soon as there's an internship which corresponds to your profile
  5. Go to your internship!

5 easy steps!

  2. Go to your profile and create your first internship
  3. Fill in all the internship info
  4. Enjoy our fun facts on the front page while we look for a student with a profile that matches your needs
  5. Teach a student lots of useful things during the internship!
